About Me

My Journey

Hello! My name is Jonathan Nguyen and I'm a senior Cinema & Media Studies and Biology double major at Carleton College aspiring to pursue a healthcare career in medicine.

I enjoy filmmaking and am interested in exploring how I can use videography as a medium for community engagement. As a first-generation low-income student, I hope to address inequities in healthcare and in higher education. I am committed to making an impact on those around me and living a life of service.

This website showcases some of my video projects and other works. I am passionate about creating and sharing authentic stories.
